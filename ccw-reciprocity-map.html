Quick Start Guide

How to Use the Map

Three clicks to know where you can legally carry. Here's everything you need to get the most out of the reciprocity map above.

1
Click Your Permit State

Click directly on the state where your concealed carry permit was issued. This sets it as your "home" state and instantly colors the entire map. You can also use the dropdown in the header.

Example — selecting Florida:

Florida (FL) — Permit State

The sidebar fills with your state's permit details, stats, and categorized state lists.

2
Read the Color-Coded Map

Every state lights up showing whether they honor your permit, offer constitutional carry, have restrictions, or don't recognize your permit at all.

Hover over any state to see a quick tooltip with its status and basic info.

3
Click Any State for Full Details

With your permit state set, click another state to open a detail panel on the right with that state's full carry laws — permit type, constitutional carry, open carry, training, duty to inform, and official resource links.

5
Switch or Reset Anytime

Click your selected permit state again to deselect and reset the map. Or use the "← Back to Overview" button in the sidebar. You can also click "Set as My Permit State" in any state's detail panel to switch on the fly.

What the Colors Mean
Cyan — Your State
The state where your permit was issued. Your home base on the map.
Green — Honors Your Permit
Recognizes your CCW permit. You can carry legally, but follow their state laws.
Teal — Constitutional Carry
No permit required. Anyone legally allowed to possess a firearm can carry concealed.
Yellow — Restricted / Partial
May honor your permit with additional conditions. Research carefully before carrying.
Red — Does Not Honor
Does not recognize your permit. Do not carry without their state-specific license.
Grey — Default
No permit state selected yet. Click a state on the map to get started.
Pro Tips
🔗
Check official sources. Always verify reciprocity with the destination state's official website before traveling. Laws change frequently.
📋
Know the local rules. Even in states that honor your permit, you must follow their laws — magazine limits, ammo restrictions, prohibited locations, and signage rules.
🛣️
Plan your route. If driving through multiple states, check each one along your path. A single "no honor" state can mean serious legal trouble.
📱
Use the dropdown too. On mobile or smaller screens, the header dropdown is the fastest way to select your permit state.
