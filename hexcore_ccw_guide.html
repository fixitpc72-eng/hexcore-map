Frequently Asked Questions

Do all states require a permit?

No. Some states allow permitless carry, but may still issue permits for reciprocity or other legal reasons.

Can I carry as soon as I apply?

No. In general, you should not carry concealed until you have satisfied your state’s legal requirements and, where required, received your permit.

Is training always required?

No. Training requirements vary by state, but quality instruction is still recommended even where it is not required.

Why use official sources?

Because permit rules, fees, timelines, and application methods can change. Always verify your process with the official issuing authority.
